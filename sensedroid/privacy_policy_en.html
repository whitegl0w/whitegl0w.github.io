Privacy Policy

Sense is distributed as a free application and is intended to be used "as is"

This page is used to inform visitors about the policy regarding the collection, transfer, use of Personal Information to work with the Sense app.

If you choose to use Sense, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Collection and use of information

To obtain a list of sensors closest to the user, the application collects information about the location of the mobile device. This information is transferred for processing to the third-party service Народный мониторинг

For analytics purposes, the service "Народный мониторинг" also basic data about the device is transmitted: IP address, device ID and model, installed software version, selected time zone and system language

The app uses third party services that may collect information used to identify you. Links to the privacy policies of third party service providers used by the application:

  • Google Play Services
  • Firebase Analytics
  • Народный мониторинг
  • Huawei Media Services

Updates to this Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated. These changes take effect immediately upon posting on this page. Therefore, it is recommended that you periodically review this page for changes.
